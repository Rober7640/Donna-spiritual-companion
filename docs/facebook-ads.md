# Facebook Ads — Benedara (Donna)

Cold traffic acquisition using the **3am moment angle**. Written to sound like a friend telling you about someone, not a company selling something.

**Target audience:** Catholic/Christian women 45-75, carrying unseen burdens (estranged adult children, aging parents, marriage stress, faith doubt, caregiver exhaustion)

**Goal:** Drive to questionnaire → start conversation with Donna

**Voice:** Personal, intimate, recognition-based ("I see you"). Never corporate. Never salesy.

## Ad Format Notes

### Facebook Ad Anatomy
- **Primary text:** The copy above the image (92-150 words ideal)
- **Headline:** Bold text below image (5-10 words)
- **Description:** Gray text below headline (15-30 words)
- **CTA button:** "Learn More" (not "Sign Up" — lower friction)

### Image Direction
- **Not:** Stock photo of praying hands, serene woman with eyes closed, sunset cross
- **Yes:** Simple, clean visual. Donna's monogram (D) on sky blue gradient background. Or: dimly lit bedroom at night (suggests 3am). Or: woman's hands holding Rosary beads, soft focus.
- **Text overlay (optional):** "3am" in simple serif font. Nothing else.

### Link Destination
- **Direct to questionnaire** (2 questions → chat starts)
- **Not:** Generic landing page, "Learn More" page, blog post
- **URL tracking:** UTM parameters for each ad variant

## AD 1: The 3am Moment (Primary)

### Primary Text

You know that thing that wakes you up at 3am?

The worry about your son. The fight with your husband you can't let go of. The grief that hits when the house is quiet.

Most people would tell you to pray about it. Or wait until morning to call someone.

But what if you could talk to someone right then?

Her name is Donna. She's 67, lost a daughter to cancer, nearly lost her marriage. She's prayed through more hard nights than she can count.

Not a counselor. Not a crisis line. Just a woman who listens and prays.

If you want to talk to her: [CTA BUTTON]

Sometimes you just need someone who understands.

### Headline
When you wake up at 3am

### Description
Someone who listens and prays. Private. Confidential. No judgment.

### Why This Works
- **Opens with recognition:** "You know that thing..." = instant self-selection
- **Paints the scenario:** 3am, can't sleep, carrying something heavy = visceral
- **Contrasts with alternatives:** "pray about it" / "wait til morning" = what they're already doing that isn't enough
- **Introduces Donna credibly:** Age, specific losses, "prayed through more hard nights" = she's been there
- **Removes barriers:** "Not a counselor. Not a crisis line." = clears confusion
- **Soft CTA:** "If you want..." = permission, not pressure
- **Closes with validation:** "Sometimes you just need someone who understands" = you're not weak for needing this

## Testing Strategy

### Week 1: Broad Recognition
Run **Ad 1 (3am)** and **Ad 2 (Invisible Burden)** in parallel.
- Test which emotion pulls better: recognition vs. validation
- Track: CTR, cost per conversation start, conversion rate from questionnaire to chat

### Week 2: Permission & Clarity
Add **Ad 3 (Permission)** and **Ad 4 (Category Breaker)**.
- Permission angle targets women with faith doubt / anger
- Category breaker targets confused clickers (saw it, didn't understand it)

### Week 3+: Scenario Targeting
Launch **Ad 5 (Estranged Child)** + variants.
- Create one ad per major pain point (estrangement, caregiver, marriage, doubt)
- Run to hyper-targeted audiences (caregiver support groups, grief support groups, etc.)

### What to Track
- **CTR (Click-Through Rate):** Which hook stops the scroll?
- **Questionnaire completion:** Did they answer both questions or drop off?
- **Conversation starts:** Did they actually talk to Donna?
- **Cost per conversation:** What are you paying to get someone into chat?

**Success benchmark:** >2% CTR, >60% questionnaire completion, >80% start conversation

## What to NEVER Say in Ads

Per Facebook ad policy + brand voice:

### Avoid (Policy Violations)
- ❌ "24/7" / "anytime" / "available now" — sounds like crisis line
- ❌ "AI" / "chatbot" / "artificial intelligence" — kills trust
- ❌ "Therapy" / "counseling" / "mental health treatment" — medical claims
- ❌ "Cure" / "heal" / "fix" — medical/religious claims
- ❌ "You" directly in targeting ("Are you depressed?") — FB blocks

### Avoid (Brand Voice)
- ❌ "Introducing" / "Excited to announce" — corporate language
- ❌ "Sign up" / "Join now" / "Get started" — transactional
- ❌ "Revolutionary" / "life-changing" / "transform" — hype
- ❌ "Don't wait" / "Limited time" / "Act now" — false urgency
- ❌ Emoji — Donna doesn't use emoji
- ❌ "Click here" — treat them like adults

### Say Instead
- ✅ "Talk to her" / "If you want to meet her"
- ✅ "She listens. She prays."
- ✅ "Private. Confidential. No judgment."
- ✅ "Sometimes you just need someone who understands."

## Compliance Notes

### Facebook Ad Review
These ads will trigger manual review because:
- Religious content (prayer, Catholic, Rosary)
- Emotional/sensitive topics (grief, estrangement, crisis)
- Health-adjacent (mental/emotional wellbeing)

**To pass review:**
- ✅ Never claim to treat, cure, or diagnose
- ✅ Never target by health condition
- ✅ Include disclaimer: "Not a substitute for professional counseling"
- ✅ Link to real product (questionnaire → chat), not just opt-in page

### Targeting Recommendations
**Do target:**
- Interest: Catholic faith, Marian devotion, prayer groups, Rosary
- Interest: Caregiver support, grief support, family relationships
- Interest: Christian parenting, Catholic women's ministry
- Age: 45-75 (demo sweet spot)
- Behavior: Engaged shoppers (willing to pay for services)

**Don't target:**
- Health conditions (depression, anxiety, grief) — FB blocks this
- Life events (recent loss, divorce) — feels predatory + blocks
- Specific vulnerability markers — ethical + policy issue

## Budget & Pacing

### Soft Launch (Week 1-2)
- **Budget:** $30-50/day total
- **Allocation:** $15/day per ad (test 2 ads)
- **Goal:** Find which hook works, validate CTR + conversion
- **Expected:** 300-500 clicks, 180-300 questionnaire starts, 150-250 conversations

### Scale (Week 3+)
- **Budget:** $100-200/day
- **Allocation:** 70% to winning ad, 30% to tests
- **Goal:** Drive volume at profitable cost per conversation
- **Expected:** 1,000-2,000 clicks/week, 600-1,200 conversations

**Success metric:** If cost per conversation < $3-5, scale aggressively.
